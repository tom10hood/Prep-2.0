Zen Gardening was created in the 6th century by Buddhist monks to help them reach deeper levels during medetation. They later used these gardens to help teach the principles and and concepts of Zen. The main parts of a Zen Garden are carefully raked sand or dirt juxtiposed with carefully splaced rocks. Plants can be added to a Zen Garden but they must be minimalist using lowspread plants instead of upright growing plants.

I created this page to express my love for Zen Gardening. Zen Gardening is something that can decrease stress and increase your quality of life. It changed my life after I got out of prison!
